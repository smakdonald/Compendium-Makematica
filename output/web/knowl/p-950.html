The automorphism group of \(C_n\) is isomorphic to the multiplicative group of units of \(\mathbb{Z}/n\) via the map
\begin{equation*} f: (\mathbb{Z}/n^\times, \cdot) \to \operatorname{Aut}(C_n), \quad f([j]_n)=[ y\mapsto y^j]. \end{equation*}
where \(\mathbb{Z}/n^\times=\{[j]_n\mid \operatorname{gcd}(j,n)=1\}\text{.}\)
