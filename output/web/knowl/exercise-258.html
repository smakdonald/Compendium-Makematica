Checkpoint 5.40.

Let \(R\) be a PID and let \(M\) be a finitely generated \(R\)-module.
  1. Determine a generator for the principal ideal \(\ann_R(M)\) in terms of the invariant factors and the free rank of \(M\text{.}\)
  2. Determine a generator for the principal ideal \(\ann_R(M)\) in terms of the elementary divisors and the free rank of \(M\text{.}\)
