Hint 4.18.1.

Here are some options the author has recently become partial to:
  1. Semiabelian: Yes, its more letters, but so are semigroup, semisimple, semidirect, and semicircle. Imagine if we called all those things normal too! It would be a nightmare. And we would deserve it for our hubris.
  2. Ideal subgroup: Look, its shorter! Normal subgroups and ideals parallel ideas in group and ring theory, and relabeling normal groups in this way would cement these similarities.
