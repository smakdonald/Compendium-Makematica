  1. \(M\) is semisimple,
  2. very submodule of \(M\) is a summand; i.e., for every submodule \(N\) of \(M\) there is a submodule \(N'\) such that \(M=N\oplus N'\) is the internal direct sum of \(N\) and \(N'\text{,}\)
  3. every injective \(R\)-map \(i: M' \to M\) is split has a left inverse,
  4. every SES of the form \(0 \to M' \to M \to M'' \to 0\) is split exact,
  5. every surjective \(R\)-map \(p: M \to M''\) has a right inverse.
