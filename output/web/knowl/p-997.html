The proof is straightforward but a bit messy. For associativity, note that
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} (y_1,x_1) \left( (y_2, x_2) (y_3, x_3) \right) &=(y_1,x_1) (y_2\rho(x_2)(y_3), x_2x_3)\\ &=(y_1\rho(x_1)\left(y_2\rho(x_2)(y_3)\right), x_1x_2x_3)\\ &=(y_1\rho(x_1)(y_2)(\rho(x_1)\circ \rho(x_2))(y_3), x_1x_2x_3)\\ &=(y_1\rho(x_1)(y_2)\rho(x_1x_2)(y_3), x_1x_2x_3)\\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
On the other hand
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} \left( (y_1,x_1) (y_2, x_2) \right) (y_3, x_3) & = (y_1 \rho(x_1)(y_2), x_1 x_2) (y_3, x_3) \\ & = (y_1 \rho(x_1)(y_2)\rho(x_1x_2)(y_3), x_1 x_2 x_3). \end{aligned} \end{equation*}
This gives associativity.
