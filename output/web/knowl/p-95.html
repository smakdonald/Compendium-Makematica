After stumbling around with your newfound power for some time, you might chance upon the fact that while your power may be unlimited, its impacts may be very limited indeed. For example, no matter how high you climb into the natural numbers you will always find that \(e^n=e\text{.}\) It seems the identity element is immune to corruption by your power, which one should probably find reassuring. Still, this raises questions that need answering, and thus we will need to reach in and pluck order out of the chaos.
