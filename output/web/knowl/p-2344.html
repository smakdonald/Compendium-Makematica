Suppose that \(V = F^m = W\text{,}\) and let \(A, B \in \M_m(F)\) be the matrices representing the linear transformations \(a\) and \(b\text{,}\) respectively, in the standard basis of \(F^m\text{.}\) Show that there is an \(F[x]\)-module isomorphism \(V_a \cong W_b\) if and only if the matrices \(A\) and \(B\) are similar.
