Let \(k = \mathbb{C}\) and consider the alternating group \(G = A_4\) of order 12. We find all the simple modules over the ring \(\mathbb{C}[A_4]\) or, equivalently, all irreducible \(\mathbb{C}\)-linear representations of \(A_4\text{.}\) We also find the Artin-Wedderburn decomposition of \(\mathbb{C}[A_4]\text{.}\)
