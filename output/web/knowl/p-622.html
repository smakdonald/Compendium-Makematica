Let \(R\) be a left semisimple ring and write \(R = I_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus I_m\) as an internal direct sum with \(I_1, \dots, I_m\) minimal left ideals. Let \(J_1, \dots, J_n\) be a complete list of representatives of isomorphism classes as left \(R\)-modules taken from the list \(I_1, \dots, I_m\text{;}\) so, for each \(i\) with \(1 \leq i \leq m\text{,}\) there is a unique \(j\) with \(1 \leq j \leq l\) so that \(I_i \cong J_j\) as left \(R\)-modules.
