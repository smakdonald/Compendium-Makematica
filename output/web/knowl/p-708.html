  1. Then \(G\) acts on \(G\) by Conjugation. For all \(g \in G\text{,}\) the orbit of \(g\) is the conjugacy class of \(g\text{,}\) \(\operatorname{Stab}_G(g)=Z_G(G)\) and \(|C_G(g)| = [G : Z_G(g)]\text{.}\)
