If \(A\) is not invertible, neither is \(AB\text{,}\) since \(\im(AB) \subseteq \im(A)\text{,}\) and if \(B\) is not invertible, neither is is \(AB\text{,}\) since \(\ker(AB) \supseteq \ker(A)\text{.}\) So, by Corollary , if either \(A\) or \(B\) is not invertible, both sides of the equation are \(0\text{.}\)
