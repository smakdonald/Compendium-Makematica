Let \(G\) be a group acting on a set \(S\text{.}\) Then
  1. \(\sim_G\) is an equivalence relation;
  2. for any \(s,s'\in S\text{,}\) either \(\operatorname{Orbit}_G(s)=\operatorname{Orbit}_G(s')\) or \(\operatorname{Orbit}_G(s)\cap \operatorname{Orbit}_G(s')=\emptyset\text{;}\) and
  3. \(S=\bigcup_{\text{pairwise disjoint orbits}} \operatorname{Orbit}_G(s)\text{.}\)
