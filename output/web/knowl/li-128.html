Item 2.

If \(V=R^n\) is free, then \(\operatorname{Aut}_R(V) \cong \operatorname{GL}_n(R)\text{,}\) where \(\operatorname{GL}_n(R)\) is the group of \(n\times n\) invertible matrices with entries in \(R\text{.}\) By a slight abuse of notation, we will say that a group homomorphism \(G\to \operatorname{GL}_n(V)\) is a representation of \(G\text{.}\)
