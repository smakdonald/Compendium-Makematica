Emmy Noether.

Emmy Noether (1882-1935) was a German mathematician who made groundbreaking contributions to the fields of algebra and theoretical physics. Born in Erlangen, Germany, Noether was the daughter of a mathematics professor and grew up with a strong interest in the subject.
