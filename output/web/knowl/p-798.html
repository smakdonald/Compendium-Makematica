If \(R\) is an integral domain and \(S\) is a multiplicatively closed subset of nonzerodivisors, the field of fractions \(S^{-1}R\) is the set of equivalence classes
\begin{equation*} S^{-1}R=\left\{\frac{r}{s} \mid r\in R,s\in S\right\}/\sim \end{equation*}
where the equivalence relation \(\sim\) is defined by
\begin{equation*} \frac{r}{s}\sim\frac{r'}{s'} \;\text{if and only if}\; rs'=r's. \end{equation*}
