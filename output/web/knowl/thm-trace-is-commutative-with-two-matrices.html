Theorem B.63. Trace is Commutative with Two Matrices.

If \(R\) is a commutative ring and \(X \in \Mat_{m \times n}(R)\) and \(Y \in \Mat_{n \times m}(R)\text{,}\) then \(\trace(XY) = \trace(YX)\text{.}\)
In particular, with the notation of the previous definition, \(\trace(g)\) is well-defined.
