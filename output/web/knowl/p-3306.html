By the degree formula we get \([L: \Q(i)] = 4\text{.}\) Since \(L = \Q(i)(z_1)\text{,}\) the degree of \(m_{z_1, \Q(i)}(x)\) must be \(4\text{.}\) This shows that \(x^4 - 2\) must remain irreducible as a polynomial in \(\Q(i)[x]\text{;}\) this is not obvious, but we have now proven it, and this fact will be useful in what we do next.
