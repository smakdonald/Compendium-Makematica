Item 1.
Let \(m > 0\) and \(n > 0\text{;}\) then by induction on \(n\) and the definition x^mx^n =x^{m + n}. Applying 1.1.3 we deduce that x^n =x^{-m}x^{m+n} and x^m = x^{m + n} x^{-n} . Finally inversion of the equation x^mx^n =x^{m + n} and application of 1.1.4 yield x^{-n}x^{-m} = x^{-m + (-n)}. Hence the law is established in all cases.
