Item 1.

Since \(1_R1_R=1_R\) we have \(f(1_R)f(1_R)=f(1_R)\text{,}\) thus
\begin{equation*} f(1_R)(f(1_R)-1_S)=0_S. \end{equation*}
Now either \(f(1_R)=0_S\) or \(f(1_R)-1_S=0_S\) (which yields \(f(1_R)=1_S\)) or both of these are nonzero and then they are complementary zerodivisors (in particular, \(f(1_R)\) is a zerodivisor).
