Let \(F\) be a field, \(V\) a finite dimensional \(F\)-vector space, and \(g: V \to V\) an \(F\)-linear operator. Prove that the following are equivalent for an element \(\lambda \in F\text{:}\)
  1. \(\lambda\) is an eigenvalue of \(g\text{.}\)
  2. \(\lambda\) is a root of the minimum polynomial \(\mp_g(x)\) of \(g\text{.}\)
  3. \(\lambda\) is a root of the characteristic polynomial \(\cp_g(x)\) of \(g\text{.}\) (Recall that \(\lambda \in F\) is a {} of \(F\) provided \(g(v) = \lambda \cdot v\) for some non-zero vector \(v \in V\text{.}\))
