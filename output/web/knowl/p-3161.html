If \(L\) is the splitting field over \(F\) of an irreducible polynomial \(f(x) \in \F[x]\) and if \(\a, \beta \in L\) are any two roots of \(f\text{,}\) then there is a field [[Mathematics/Definitions/Automorphism|automorphism]] \(\s: L \xra{\cong} L\) such that \(\s|_F = \id_F\) and \(\s(\a) = \beta\text{.}\)
