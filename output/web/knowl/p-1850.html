Let \(V\) be the set of all \(r \times s\) matrices over a field \(F\text{,}\) let \(G\) denote the group \(GL_r(F) \times GL_s(F)\) (where, recall, \(GL_m(F)\) is the group of \(m \times m\) invertible matrices with entries in \(F\)) and set \((A,B)\cdot M=AMB^{-1}\) for all \(M \in V\) and \((A,B) \in G\text{.}\) 1. Prove that the formula above defines a group action. 2. Prove that each orbit contains a matrix \(M = (m_{i,j})\) such that \(m_{i,j} = 0\) for all \(i \ne j\) and \(m_{i,i} \in \{0,1\}\) for all \(i\) 3. How many orbits are there?
