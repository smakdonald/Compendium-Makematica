Item 5.

The action of the group of permutations of a set \(X\) on the set of all subsets of \(X\) by permutation is both transitive and faithful. Any subset can be sent to any other subset by permuting the elements of \(X\text{,}\) and every non-identity permutation corresponds to a different permutation of the subsets of \(X\text{.}\)
