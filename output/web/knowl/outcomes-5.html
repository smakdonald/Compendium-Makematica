Summary 2.1.

  • A Subgroup is a subset of a group that is also a group under the same operation. The fastest way to show something is a subgroup is with one of the Subgroup Tests.
  • The Center of a Group is the subgroup of elements of a group that commute with every other element.  3 
  • The Kernel and image of a group homomorphism are subgroups.  4
