Proposition 4.7. Krull-Schmidt for Semisimple Modules.

Let \(M\) be a finitely generated semisimple module. Given two direct sum decompositions as simple modules
\begin{equation*} M = M_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus M_m = N_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus N_n \end{equation*}
then \(m=n\text{,}\) and there is a permutation \(\sigma\) such that \(M_{\sigma(i)} \cong N_i\) for all \(i\)
