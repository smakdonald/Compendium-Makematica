Solution G.41.1.

  1. Let \(I\) be a maximal ideal in \(R\) and consider the residue field \(R/I\text{.}\) As \(R^m\cong R^n\text{,}\) we can tensor both sides to see \(R^m\tensor I\cong R^n\tensor I\text{,}\) and so \(R^m/IR^m\cong R^n/IR^n\text{.}\) As both of these are finitely dimensional vector spaces, they must have the same rank, and thus \(m=n\text{.}\)
