Now let \(K=F(i)\text{.}\) Notice that \(i\) is a root of the polynomial \(x^2+1\text{,}\) which is irreducible in \(F[x]\) as \(F\subseteq\R\) and \(i\not\in\R\text{.}\) Thus \(x^2+1\) is monic and irreducible, making it the minimal polynomial of \(i\) Thus \([K:F]=2\) and \([K:\Q]=8\) by the The Degree Formula.
