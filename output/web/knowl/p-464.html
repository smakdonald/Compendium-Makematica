Let \(M\) be an \(R\)-module. The map
\begin{equation*} \xymatrix@R=1mm{ \operatorname{End}_R(M^{\oplus n}) \ar[r]^-{\Theta} & \operatorname{Mat}_n(\operatorname{End}_R(M)) \\ \phi \ar@{|->}[r] & [\pi_i \phi \iota_j]_{i,j} } \end{equation*}
is a ring isomorphism, where \(\iota_k\) and \(\pi_k\) denote the natural inclusion and projection maps.
