Let \(m > 0\) and \(n > 0\text{;}\) then by induction on \(n\) and the definition \(x^mx^n =x^{m + n}\text{.}\) Multiplying on both sides by \(x^{-m}\) we deduce that \(x^n =x^{-m}x^{m+n}\) and \(x^m = x^{m + n} x^{-n}\text{.}\) Finally inversion of the equation \(x^mx^n =x^{m + n}\) yields \(x^{-n}x^{-m} = x^{-m + (-n)}\text{.}\) Hence the law is established in all cases.
