Method II: Call \(d_1, d_2\) the entries on the diagonal of the SNF of \(xI_2-A\text{.}\) Recall from Theorem that \(d_1\) is the gcd of the entries of \(xI_2-A\) and \(d_1d_2=\det(xI_2-A)\text{.}\) Thus \(d_1=1\) and \(d_2=\det(xI_2-A)=(x-1)^2\text{.}\) Therefore the only invariant factor of \(A\) is \((x-1)^2\text{.}\)
