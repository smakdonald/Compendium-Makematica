Definition A.22.

Let \(A_\alpha\) for all \(\alpha \in J\) be sets and let \(\beta in J\text{.}\) Theprojection map \(p\beta : \prod_{ \alpha \in J} A_\alpha \to A_\beta\) is defined by \(p\beta ((a\alpha )\alpha \in J) = a\beta for all (a\alpha )\alpha \in J \in \prod \alpha \in J A\alpha\) .
