Consider
\begin{equation*} V=\{e, (1 2)(3 4), (1 3)(2 4), (1 4)(2 3)\}. \end{equation*}
From Exercise 3.2.1 we have \(V \nsg S_4\text{.}\) The group \(V\) has order \(4\text{,}\) making it ableian from Proposition 1.14. Thus every subgroup of \(V\) is normal in \(V\) by Exercise 3.2.1.
