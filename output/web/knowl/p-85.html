Though it might be natural to write the product \(x\cdot x\) using the notation \(x^2\text{,}\) we have no notion of what that means in the world of groups. Do exponents behave the way we want them to in groups, or does something disasterous occur? Do exponents even exist? Do I? We can answer most of these questions, and attempt to do so now.
