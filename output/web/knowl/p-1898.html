Let \(V\) be a finite dimensional vector space over a field \(F\) and \(f: V \to V\) an \(F\)-linear transformation. Prove the following assertions.
  1. There exists an integer \(s \geq 0\) such that for \(n \geq s\) there are equalities \(\ker(f^n) = \ker(f^s)\) and \(\im(f^n) = \im(f^s)\text{.}\) (Here \(f^m\) denotes \(f\) composed with itself \(m\) times.)
  2. \(\ker(f^s) \cap \im(f^s) = \{0\}\) for any \(s\) as above.
  3. \(V = \ker(f^s) \oplus \im(f^s)\) for any s as above. (Recall \(V = U \oplus W\) for subspaces \(U\) and \(W\) if \(U + W = V\) and \(U\cap W = \{0\}\text{.}\) )
