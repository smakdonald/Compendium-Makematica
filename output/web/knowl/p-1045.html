The actions \(\rho\) and \(\rho'\) are not identical. For example, assuming \(n\geq 4\) we have
\begin{equation*} \rho(x)(1 \, 2) (1 \, 2) (3 \, 4) = (1 \, 2) (3 \, 4) \text { maps } 1\mapsto 2 \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \rho'(x)(1 \, 2) (3 \, 4) = (1 \, 3) (1 \, 2) (3 \, 4) (1 \, 3) \text { maps } 1\mapsto 4. \end{equation*}
Yet
\begin{equation*} A_n \rtimes_{\rho} H \cong A_n \rtimes_{\rho'} H' \end{equation*}
since each is isomorphic to \(S_n\text{.}\)
