Solution 7.1.3.1.
  1. Let \(A,B\) be \(n\times n\) matrices.
  2. Let \(A\) be a matrix in \(\C\text{.}\) Eigenvalues are the roots of \(\cp_A(x)\text{,}\) which is the determinant of the matrix \(A-xI\text{.}\) Every matrix \(A\) is similar to a matrix in Rational Canonical Form, and similar matrices share invariant factors and thus characteristic polynomials, the roots of which are the eigenvalues of the matrix.
