Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Consider the real vector space \(V = \{p \in R[x] | \deg(p) \leq n\}\) and the [[Mathematics/Definitions/Linear Transformation|linear transformation]] \(T : V \to V, T (p) = p'\text{,}\) where \(p'(x)\) is the [[Mathematics/Definitions/Derivative|derivative]] of p(x).
