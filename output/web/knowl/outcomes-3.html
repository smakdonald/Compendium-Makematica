Summary 1.3.

  • The Dihedral Group is the group of reflections and rotations of a regular polygon. Every element in \(D_{2n}\) can be written as a product of rotations and reflections.  2 
  • The Definition 1.38 \(S_n\) is set of all Permutations of a set with \(n\) elements. The order of \(S_n\) is \(n!\text{.}\)  3 
  • Permutations are either even or odd; the Alternating Group is the set of even permutations. The order of \(A_n\) is \(\frac{n!}{2}\text{.}\)  4 
  • Cycles and Transpositions are special permutations. Every cycle can be written as a product of transpositions.  5 
  • The Quaternions exist.
