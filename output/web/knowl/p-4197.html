Let \(G\) be a group of order \(p^mq\) where \(p\) and \(q\) are distinct primes and \(m \geq 1\text{.}\) Assume that \(G\) has at least one element of order \(p^m\text{.}\) Let \(S\) be the set of all elements of \(G\) of order \(p^m\text{,}\) that is \(S = \{x \in G : |x| = p^m\}\text{.}\) (a) Prove that \(G\) acts on \(S\) by conjugation. (b) Prove that if \(Z(G) = \{e\}\) then \(q\) divides \(|S|\text{.}\)
