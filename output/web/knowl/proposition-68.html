Proposition 1.47.

Consider the two field extensions \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt{3})\) and \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt[3]{2})\text{.}\)
  1. Show that \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt{3})\) has degree \(4\text{.}\)
  2. Show that \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt[3]{2})\) has degree \(6\text{.}\)
  3. Find a primitive element \(\gamma\) for the extension \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt{3})\text{.}\)
  4. Find \(m_{\gamma, \Q}(x)\text{.}\)
