Theorem 7.6. Direct Products and Homomorphisms.

  1. Let \(G\) and \(H\) be groups. Then the projection map \(\pi_G:G\times H\to G\) is a surjective homomorphism of groups.
  2. Let \(G\) and \(H\) be groups. Then the inclusion map \(\iota_G:G\to G\times H\) is an injective homomorphism of groups.
