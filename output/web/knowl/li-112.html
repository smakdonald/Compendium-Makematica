Item 1.
\(\Rightarrow\) is immediate from the definitions (since \(e_G \in \operatorname{ker}(f)\) for all homomorphisms \(f\)). If \(\operatorname{ker}(f) = \{e_G\}\) and \(f(h) = f(h')\) then \(f(h^{-1}h') = f(h)^{-1}f(h') = e_H\) and thus \(h^{-1}h' = e_G\) which implies \(h = h'\text{.}\)
