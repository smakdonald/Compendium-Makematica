Item 3.

We proceed by induction on length again. Given a filtration of \(M\text{,}\) we can suppose that there is some nonzero proper submodule \(L\) in the filtration, since otherwise we could just take any composition series. Then \(L\) and \(\overline{M}\) has length less than \(M\text{.}\) The filtration up to \(L\) can be refined to a strict composition series by the induction hypothesis, and the filtration from \(L\) to \(M\) taken mod \(L\) can be refined to a strict composition series for \(\overline{M}\text{;}\) pulling back as in the remark above, we get the strict composition series we want.
