Theorem 12.15. Classification Theorem.

Let \(F\) be a field.
  1. Every finitely generated vector space over \(F\) is isomorphic to \(F^{n}\) for \(n=\operatorname{dim}_{F}(V)\text{.}\)
  2. For any \(m, n \in \mathbb{Z}_{\geqslant 0}, F^{m} \cong F^{n}\) if and only if \(m=n\text{.}\)
