Problem 6.13. June 2014 (3).

Let \(G\) be a group that acts on a set \(A\text{,}\) and \(H\) a subgroup of \(G\) such that for any \(a, b \in A\) there exists a unique \(h \in H\) with \(ha = b\text{.}\)
  1. Prove that for every \(a \in A, G = HG_a\) and \(H \cap Ga = \{1\}\text{,}\) where \(G_a = \{g \in G | ga = a\}.\)
  2. Prove that if \(H \subseteq Z(G)\) then for every \(a \in A\text{,}\) \(G\) is the internal direct product of \(H\) and \(G_a.\)
