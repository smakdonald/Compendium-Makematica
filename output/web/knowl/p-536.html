Let \(G\) be the group of all \(2\times2\) matrices with entres from \(\Z\) having determinant \(1\text{.}\) Let \(p\) be a prime number, and take \(K\) to be the subset of \(G\) consisting of all \(\begin{bmatrix} \end{bmatrix}\) with \(a\equiv d\equiv 1\mod p\) and \(b\equiv c\equiv 0\mod p\text{.}\) Prove that \(K\) is a normal subgroup of \(G\text{.}\)
