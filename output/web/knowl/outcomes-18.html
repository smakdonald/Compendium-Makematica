Summary 6.2.

  • External Semidirect Products are defined using homomorphisms into automorphism groups.  2  They are often used to construct non-abelian groups.
  • Internal Semidirect Products are similar to their direct counterparts, though only require that one of \(H\) or \(K\) be normal. They can be identified using the Recognition Theorem for Internal Semidirect Products.
  • Using semidirect products, we are able to provide a Classification for Groups of Order \(pq\).
