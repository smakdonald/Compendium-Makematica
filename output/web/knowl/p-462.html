Let \(H \leq G\text{.}\) The following facts about left cosets are equivalent for \(x,y \in G\text{:}\)
  1. \(x\) and \(y\) belong to the same left coset of \(H\) in \(G\text{,}\)
  2. \(x = yh\) for some \(h \in H\text{,}\)
  3. \(y = xh\) for some \(h \in H\text{,}\)
  4. \(y^{-1}x \in H\text{,}\)
  5. \(x^{-1}y \in H\text{,}\)
  6. \(xH = yH\text{.}\)
