Let \(R\) be a domain and let \(F\) be its fraction field. Show that \(F\) has the following universal property: if \(K\) is any field and \(f\!: R \to K\) is any injective ring homomorphism, then \(f\) extends to an injective ring homomorphism \(\overline{f}\!: F \to K\text{,}\) so that \(\overline{f}|_R=f\text{.}\)
