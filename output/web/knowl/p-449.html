We conclude that every real representation of \(C_3\) is isomorphic to a direct sum of copies of the trivial representation and copies of \(V/W\text{.}\) That is, for any such representation \(U\text{,}\) there is a basis of \(U\text{,}\) \(\{e_\alpha \}_{\alpha\in A}\text{,}\) \(\{e'_\beta, e''_\beta\}_{\beta\in B}\text{,}\) such that \(g\cdot e_\alpha = e_\alpha\text{,}\) \(g\cdot e'_\beta = e''_\beta\text{,}\) and \(g\cdot e''_\beta = - e'_\beta - e''_\beta\text{.}\)
