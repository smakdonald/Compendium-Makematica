Let \(G\) be the group of rotatoinal symmetries of the dodecahedron (\(12\) pentagonal faces). The evident action of \(G\) on the twleve faces is transitive. For each face \(f\text{,}\) \(G_f\) clearly has \(5\) elements: the five rotations about the line joining the midpoint of \(f\) to the midpoint of the face on the opposite side. Since \(12 = [G: G_f]\) and \(\# G_f = 5\text{,}\) we conclude \(\# G = 60\text{.}\)
