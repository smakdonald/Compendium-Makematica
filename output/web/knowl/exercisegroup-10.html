Consider the set \(\mu=\{z\in \C^\times \mid z^n = 1 \;\text{for some integer}\;n \geq 1\}\text{.}\)

  2. Prove that for each integer \(m \geq 1\text{,}\) there is a unique subgroup \(H_m\leq \mu\) with \(|H_m|=m\) and that \(H_m\) is cyclic.
  3. Prove that every finitely generated subgroup of \(\mu\) has finitely many elements.
  4. Prove that \(\mu\) is not finitely generated.
