Let \(V, W\) be finitely generated free modules over a commutative ring \(R\text{,}\) let \(B\) and \(B^{\prime}\) be bases of \(V\text{,}\) let \(C\) and \(C^{\prime}\) be bases of \(W\text{,}\) and let \(f: V \rightarrow W\) be a homomorphism. Then
\begin{equation*} [f]_{B^{\prime}}^{C^{\prime}}=\left[\mathrm{id}_{W}\right]_{C}^{C^{\prime}}[f]_{B}^{C}\left[\mathrm{id}_{V}\right]_{B^{\prime}}^{B} \end{equation*}
In particular, if \(g: V \rightarrow V\) is an \(R\)-module homomorphism, then \([g]_{B}^{B}\) and \([g]_{B^{\prime}}^{B^{\prime}}\) are similar.
