Definition 14.30. Change of Basis Matrix.

Let \(V\) be a finite rank free module over a commutative ring \(R\text{,}\) and let \(B\) and \(B'\) be bases of \(V\text{.}\) Let \(\id_V\) be the identity map on \(V\text{.}\) Then \([\id_V]_B^{B'}\) is called the change of basis matrix from \(B\) to \(B'.\)
