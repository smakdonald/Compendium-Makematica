Let \(A\alpha\) for all \(\alpha \in J\) be sets, let \(\beta \in J\text{,}\) and for each \(\alpha \in J - \{\beta \}\text{,}\) let \(b\alpha \in A\alpha\text{.}\) The associated product inclusionmap \(i\beta : A\beta \to \prod \alpha \in J A\alpha\) is defined for each \(a \in A\beta\) by \(i\beta (a) := (a\alpha )\alpha \in J\text{,}\) where \(a\beta := a and a\alpha := b\alpha for all \alpha \in J - \{\beta \}\text{.}\)
