Let \(P\) be a Sylow \(2\)-subgroup of \(\Gal(K/F)\text{.}\) Notice that \(|P|=4\text{.}\) By the FTGT there exists an intermediate field extension \(E\) such that \(|\Gal(K/E)|=4\text{.}\) By the Degree Formula, we have \([K:F]=[K:E][E:F]\text{,}\) with \([K:F]=20\) and \([K:L]=4\text{.}\) Thus \([L:F]=5\text{.}\)
