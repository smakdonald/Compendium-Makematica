Consider the matrix
\begin{equation*} A=\begin{bmatrix} x & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & x & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & x-1 \end{bmatrix} \in \M_{3,3}(R), \end{equation*}
where \(R=\Q[x]\text{.}\)
  1. Determine the Smith normal form for \(A\text{.}\)
  2. Determine the representative in the isomorphism class of the module presented by \(A\) which is written in invariant factor form and in elementary divisor form.
