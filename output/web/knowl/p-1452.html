(Second Isomorphism Theorem) Let \(A\) and \(B\) be submodules of \(M\text{,}\) and define \(A + B = \{a+b \mid a \in A, b \in B\}\text{.}\) Then \(A + B\) is a submodule of \(M\text{,}\) \(A \cap B\) is a submodule of \(A\text{,}\) and there is an \(R\)-module isomorphism \((A + B)/B \cong A/(A \cap B)\text{.}\)
