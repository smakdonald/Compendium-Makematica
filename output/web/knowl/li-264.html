Item 1.
Recall from [provisional cross-reference: cite] that the set of left cosets of a subgroup \(H\) induce an equivalence relation on \(G\text{.}\) This is the same equivalence relation induced by \(G\) acting upon itself by Left Multiplication. Thus, under this action, \(\Orb_G(g)=gH\text{.}\)
