\(\defn\) – Separable (Polynomial).

Let \(F\) be a field and \(f(x)\) a polynomial in \(F[x]\)[^1]. If the [[Mathematics/Definitions/Multiplicity|multiplicity]] of every root is \(1\text{,}\) we say \(f(x)\) is a \(\defnn{separable polynomial}\text{;}\) i.e. \(f\) is separable provided it has no repeated roots.
