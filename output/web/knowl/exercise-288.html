Exercise 11. .
Let \(n\) be a positive integer. Consider the real vector space \(V = \{p \in R[x] | \deg(p) \leq n\}\) and the linear transformation \(T : V \to V, T (p) = p'\text{,}\) where \(p'(x)\) is the derivative of p(x).
  1. Find the characteristic polynomial and the minimum polynomial for \(T\text{.}\)
  2. Find the invariant factors and the elementary divisors for \(T\text{.}\)
  3. Find the RCF and the Jordan Canonical Form for \(T\text{.}\)
