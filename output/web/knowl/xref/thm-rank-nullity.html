Theorem 13.55. Rank-Nullity Theorem.

Let \(F\) be a field and \(f: V \to W\) an \(F\)-linear transformation between \(F\)-vector spaces \(V\) and \(W\text{,}\) and assume \(V\) is finite dimensional. Then
\begin{equation*} \dim(V) = \rank(f) + \null(f) \end{equation*}
or equivalently
\begin{equation*} \dim(V) = \dim(\ker(f)) + \dim(\im(f)). \end{equation*}

Proof.

By the First Isomorphism Theorem for Modules we have \(V/\ker(f)\cong\im(f)\text{,}\) thus \(\dim\left(V/\ker(f)\right)=\dim(\im(f))\text{.}\) By TheoremÂ 13.52 we have
\begin{equation*} \dim(V)=\dim(\ker(f)) \dim\left(V/\ker(f)\right) =\dim(\ker(V))+\dim(\im(f)). \end{equation*}
