Example 1.62. Isomorphism Examples.

  1. The identity map is a group isomomorphism for any group \(G\text{.}\)
  2. \((\Z,+)\cong(\E,+)\text{,}\) where \((\E,+)\) is as defined in Exercise 1.14.
  3. \(\displaystyle (\R,+)\cong(\R^\times,\cdot)\)
  4. The exponential and natural logarithm maps from Example 1.53 are inverses, making them isomorphisms as well.
