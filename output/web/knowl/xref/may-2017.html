5. Problem 5.

Let \(F\) be a field and \(f(x) \in F[x]\) a monic polynomial of degree \(n\text{.}\) Prove: all matrices in \(\mathcal{M}_{n \times n}(F)\) having characteristic polynomial \(f(x)\) are similar if and only if the irreducible factorization of \(f(x)\) has no repeated factors.
Solution.

6. Problem 6.

  2. Make \(\mathbb{R}^{3}\) into a \(\mathbb{R}[x]\)-module by letting \(f(x) \mathbf{v}=f(A) \mathbf{v}\) for any vector \(\mathbf{v}\) in \(\mathbb{R}^{3}\text{,}\) where \(A\) is the matrix

Solution.
