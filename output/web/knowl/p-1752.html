Does \(x^4 + 8x^3 + 21x^2 + 20x + 13 \in \Q[x]\) have any roots? No. The only possible roots are \(\pm 1\) and \(\pm 13\text{,}\) and a careful check rules these out. Is \(x^4 + 8x^3 + 21x^2 + 20x + 13 \in \Q[x]\) irreducible? No, it factors as \(x^2 + x + 1\) times \(x^2 + 7x + 13\)
