Let \(F\) be a field and let \(A \in \Mat_{n \times n}(F)\text{.}\) The \(\defnn{minimum polynomial}\) of \(A\text{,}\) denoted \(\mp_A(x)\text{,}\) is the unique monic generator of the ideal \(\{g(x) \in F[x] \, \mid \, g(A) = 0\}\text{.}\) Equivalently, \(\mp_A(x)\) is the monic polynomial of least degree such that \(\mp_A(A) = 0\text{.}\)
