Let \(V, W\) be finitely generated free modules over a commutative ring \(R\text{,}\) let \(B\) and \(B'\) be bases of \(V\text{,}\) let \(C\) and \(C'\) be bases of \(W\text{,}\) and let \(f: V \to W\) be a homomorphism. Then
\begin{equation*} \begin{equation}[f]_{B'}^{C'} = [\id_W]_C^{C'} [f]_B^C [\id_V]_{B'}^{B}.\end{equation} \end{equation*}
