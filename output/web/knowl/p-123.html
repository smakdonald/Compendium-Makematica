The exponential map \(\exp: ({\mathbb{R}}, +) \to ({\mathbb{R}}\setminus \{0\}, \cdot)\) is a homomorphism. So is \(\ln: ({\mathbb{R}}_{>0}, \cdot) \to ({\mathbb{R}}, +)\text{.}\) In fact, these maps are inverse to each other so we obtain an isomorphism . \(({\mathbb{R}}, +)\cong ({\mathbb{R}}_{>0}, \cdot)\text{.}\)
