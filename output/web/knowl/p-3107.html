  1. Prove there are \(d\) distinct elements in the set \(\{\s(\alpha) | \sigma \in G\}\text{.}\)
  2. Prove
    \begin{equation*} \prod_{\s\in G} (x-\s(\alpha))=m(x)^{n/d} \end{equation*}
    ##### Proof. Let \(K/F\) be a finite Galois field extension, \(G = Gal(K/F)\text{,}\) and \(n = |G|\text{.}\) Let \(\alpha\) be an element of \(K\) and \(m(x)\) its minimal polynomial over \(F\) ; set \(d = \deg m(x)\text{.}\)
