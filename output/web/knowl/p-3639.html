Hilbert was born in Königsberg, Germany (now Kaliningrad, Russia), and he studied mathematics at the University of Königsberg. After completing his studies, he worked as a professor of mathematics at several universities in Germany, including the University of Göttingen, where he spent most of his career. In addition to his research, Hilbert was an influential teacher and mentor, and he played a key role in the development of mathematical education in Germany.
