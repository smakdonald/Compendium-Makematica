Let \(\alpha,\beta\in\Z[\sqrt{10}]\) and behold
\begin{equation*} \begin{align}N(\alpha\beta)&=N((a+b\sqrt{10})(c+d\sqrt{10}))\\ &=N(ac+10bd+(ad+bc)(\sqrt{10}))\\ &=(ac+10bd)^2-10(ad+bc)^2\\ &=((ac)^2+20acbd+100(bd)^2)+(-10(ad)^2-20abcd-10(bc)^2)\\ &=a^2c^2+100b^2d^2-10a^2d^2-10b^2c^2\\ &=(a^2c^2-10a^2d^2)+(-10b^2c^2-100b^2d^2)\\ &=a^2(c^2-10d^2)-10b^2(c^2-10d^2)\\ N(\alpha)N(\beta)&=(a^2-10b^2)(c^2-10d^2).\end{align} \end{equation*}
So there's that done.
