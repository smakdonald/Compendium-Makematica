  1. The direct product of groups is abelian if and only if every factor of the product is abelian.
  2. Let \(G\) be a direct product of groups. If \(H\) is a direct product of subgroups of \(G\text{,}\) then \(H\leq G\text{.}\)
  3. If \(G_1\cong G_2\) and \(H_1\cong H_2\text{,}\) then \(G_1\times H_1\cong G_2\times H_2\text{.}\)
  4. Let \(g_1\times\cdots\times g_n\in G_1\times\cdots\times G_n\text{.}\) Then \(|g_1\times\cdots\times g_n|=\lcm(|g_1|,\dots,|g_n|)\text{.}\)
