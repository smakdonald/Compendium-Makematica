Let \(a+b\sqrt d\in\)\(\Q(\sqrt d)\text{,}\) and notice that \(f(a+bx)=a+b\sqrt d\text{,}\) giving us surjectivity. Also,
\begin{equation*} f((a+b\sqrt d)(p+q\sqrt d))=(a+bx)(p+qx)=f(a+b\sqrt d)f(p+q\sqrt d) \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} f((a+b\sqrt d)+(p+q\sqrt d))=(a+bx)+(p+qx)=f(a+b\sqrt d)+f(p+q\sqrt d), \end{equation*}
making \(f\) a homomorphism as well.
