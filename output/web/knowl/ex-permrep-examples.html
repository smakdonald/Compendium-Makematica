Example 5.7. Common Permutation Representations.

  1. For the trivial action, the associated group homomorphism is \(G \to \operatorname{Perm}(S)\) by \(g\mapsto \text{id}_S\)
  2. For left multiplication, the associated group homomorphism is \(G \to \operatorname{Perm}(S)\) by \(g\mapsto \phi_g\text{,}\) where \(\phi_g(s)=gs\)
  3. For the conjugation action, the associated group homomorphism is \(G \to \operatorname{Perm}(S)\) by \(g\mapsto \psi_g\text{,}\) where \(\psi_g(s)=gsg\inv\)
