Consider \(x^6-4\text{,}\) and note that \(\gamma^2=2^{2/3}\text{,}\) and thus \(\gamma^6=2^2=4\text{.}\) Thus \(\gamma^6-4=0\text{,}\) so \(\gamma\) is a root of this polynomial. Since this is a monic polynomial of degree \(6=[L(i):\Q]\text{,}\) it must be the minimal polynomial as well. Thus the minimal polynomial of \([L(i):\Q]=x^6-4\text{.}\)
