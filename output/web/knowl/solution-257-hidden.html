Proof. Let \(p\) be a prime and \(H\) a finite \(p\)-group. Part (a) Suppose \(H\) acts on the finite set \(S\text{,}\) and let \(S_0\) be the fixed points of the action. Let \(x\in S_0\text{,}\) and note that \(\Stab_H(x)=H\text{.}\) By Orbit-Stabilizer we see \(|\Orb_H(x)|=1\text{.}\)
The orbits of an action partition the set \(S\text{,}\) and as orbits are either disjoint or equal, we see \(|S|=\sum\limits_{x\in S}|\Orb_H(s)|\text{.}\)
