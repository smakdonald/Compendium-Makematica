Remark 2.12.

Moving forward, we will essentially treat Theorem 2.11 as the de-facto definition of a group isomorphism. Rather than end every proof with a reference to Theorem 2.11, we will instead enshrine its monumental achievements of Theorem 2.11 within this remark. To alleviate some of the corresponding guilt asociated with this decision I have am referencing Theorem 2.11 as many times as possible, as Theorem 2.11 is important and Theorem 2.11 should not be forgotten.
