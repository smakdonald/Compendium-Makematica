Item 20.2.15.a.
Notice \(\zeta\) is a root of the polynomial \(x^{17}-1\text{,}\) and we can factor out an \((x-1)\) to see that \(\zeta\) is a root of the cyclotomic polynomial \(f(x)=x^{16}+x^{15}+\dots+x+1\text{.}\) Thanks to Mark, this is a monic irreducible polynomial in \(\Q[x]\text{,}\) making \(f\) the minimal polynomial of \(\zeta\text{,}\) and thus the degree of the extension is \(16\text{.}\)
