Proposition 1.43.

Consider the two field extensions \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt{3})\) and \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt[3]{2})\text{.}\)
  1. Prove they have degrees \(4\) and \(6\text{,}\) respectively. {}: For the second one, show \(x^3 - 2\) is irreducible in \(\Q(i)[x]\text{.}\)
  2. Find a primitive element \(\gamma\) for the extension \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt{3})\) and find \(m_{\gamma, \Q}(x)\text{.}\)
  3. Find a primitive element \(\delta\) for the extension \(\Q \subseteq \Q(i, \sqrt[3]{2})\) and find \(m_{\delta, \Q}(x)\text{.}\)
