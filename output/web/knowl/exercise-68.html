Exercise 5. June 2010 (1), January 2015 (3).
Let \(G\) be a group and \(H\) a subgroup of \(G\text{.}\) Recall that the centralizer of \(H\) in \(G\) is
\begin{equation*} Z_H(G)=\{g\in G|gh=h\;\text{for all}\;h\in H\} \end{equation*}
Prove that if \(H\) is normal in \(G\text{,}\) then so is \(Z_G(H)\) and that \(G/Z_G(H)\) is isomorphic to a subgroup of the automorphism group of \(H\text{.}\)
