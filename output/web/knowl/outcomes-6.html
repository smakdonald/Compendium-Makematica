Summary 2.2.

  • For a subset \(S\) of a group, the Generated Subgroup of \(S\) is the smallest subgroup containing \(S\text{.}\)
  • \(\langle S \rangle = \left\{x_1^{j_1} \cdots x_m^{j_m} \mid m \geq 0, j_1, \dots, j_m \in \mathbb{Z}\text{ and }x_1, \dots, x_m \in S \right\}\text{.}\)  2 
  • Two prominent examples are \(D_{2n}=\igen{r,s}\) and \(A_n=\langle (i \ j )(k \ l) \mid i,j,k,l \in \{1, \ldots, n\}\rangle\text{.}\)
