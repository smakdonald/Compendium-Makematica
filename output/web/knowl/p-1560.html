Let \(F\) be a field. Given an \(F\)-linear transformation \(g: V \to U\) between finite dimensional \(F\)-vector spaces \(V\) and \(U\text{,}\) there are bases \(B\) and \(C\) of \(V\) and \(U\text{,}\) respectively, such that
\begin{equation*} [g]_B^C = \begin{bmatrix} I_r & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \\ \end{bmatrix} \end{equation*}
where \(r = \rank(g)\) and the \(0\)'s denote appropriately sized matrices with all \(0\) entries.
