Let \(F\) be a field and consider a monic polynomial \(f(x)=x^n+a_{n-1}x^{n-1}+\cdots+a_1x+a_0\) in \(F[x]\) with \(n \geqslant 1\text{.}\)
  1. Show that the principal ideal \((f(x))\) is a subspace of the \(F\)-vector space \(F[x]\text{.}\)
  2. Show that the set \(B=\{\ov{1},\ov{x},\ldots,\ov{x^{n-1}}\}\text{,}\) where \(\ov{x^i}=x^i+(f(x))\text{,}\) is a basis for the quotient \(F\)-vector space \(F[x]/(f(x))\text{.}\)
  3. Consider the linear transformation \(\l_x : F [x]/(f (x)) \to F[x]/(f(x))\) defined by \(\l_x(v)=\ov{x}v\) for any \(v\in F [x]/(f (x))\text{.}\) Find the matrix representing \(\l_x\) in the basis \(B\) from part (b).
