Part (b) Let \(\alpha\) be a real root of \(f(x)\text{.}\) As \(f\) is irreducible this root is not in \(\Q\text{.}\) As \(\alpha\) is the root of a monic irreducible polynomial of degree \(5\text{,}\) we see that \([\Q(\alpha):\Q]=5\text{.}\) By the FTGT there exists a subgroup of \(\Gal(L/\Q)\) with order \(5\text{,}\) making it a cyclic subgroup generated by some element of order \(5\text{.}\)
