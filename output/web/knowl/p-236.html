Let \(G\) be a group and \(\operatorname{Aut}(G)\) be its automorphism group. In Problem 7 on HW # 2, you proved that for each \(g\in G\text{,}\) the function \(\psi_g:G\to G\) defined by \(\psi_g(x)=gxg^{-1}\) for all \(x\in G\) is an automorphism of \(G\text{.}\) Further, you proved the set \(\operatorname{Inn}(G):=\{\psi_g \mid g\in G\}\text{,}\) called the set of inner automorphisms of \(G\text{,}\) is a subgroup of \(\operatorname{Aut}(G)\text{.}\) Prove that \(\operatorname{Inn}(G)\triangleleft \operatorname{Aut}(G)\text{.}\)
