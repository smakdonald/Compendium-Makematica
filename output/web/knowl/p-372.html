Let \(G\) be a finite group, \(p\) a prime dividing the order of \(G\text{,}\) and \(H\) a normal subgroup of order \(p^m\) for some \(m > 0\text{.}\) Prove the following statements. (a) \(H\) is contained in every Sylow \(p\)-subgroup of \(G\text{.}\) (b) \(H\) contains a nontrivial abelian subgroup that is normal in \(G\text{.}\)
