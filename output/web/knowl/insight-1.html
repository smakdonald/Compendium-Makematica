Mark 1.5.

We say that “\(S\) is closed under the operation \(\cdot\)”, when we want to emphasize that for any \(x,y\in S\) the result of the operation, \(x*y\text{,}\) is an element of \(S\text{.}\) However note that closure is really part of the definition of a binary operation on a set, and it is implicitly assumed whenever we consider such an operation.  2
