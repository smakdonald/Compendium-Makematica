The elements of \(S_3\) are the following:
\begin{equation*} \{(1), (12), (13), (23), (123), (132)\} \end{equation*}
The subgroup \(F=\langle(123)\rangle=\{(123),(132)\}\) has index \(2\) in \(G\) and is thus normal. None of the order \(2\) subgroups are normal in \(S_3\text{,}\) so \(F\) is the only strictly intermediate extension.
