First, notice that \(2\cdot 2=6=(1+\sqrt{-3})(1-\sqrt{-3})\text{.}\) Define a function
\begin{equation*} N:\Z[\sqrt{-3}]\to\Z, N(a+b \sqrt{-3})=a^2+3b^2. \end{equation*}
Let \(\alpha,\beta\in\Z[i]\) and behold
\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned*} N(\alpha\beta) &=N((a+b \sqrt{-3})(c+d \sqrt{-3}))\\ &=N(ac-3bd+ad \sqrt{-3}+bc \sqrt{-3})\\ &=(ac-3bd)^2+3(ad+bc)^2\\ &=((ac)^2-6acbd+9(bd)^2)+3((ad)^2+2abcd+(bc^2))\\ &=a^2c^2+9b^2d^2+3a^2d^2+3b^2c^2\\ &=(a^2c^2+3a^2d^2)+(3b^2c^2+9b^2d^2)\\ &=a^2(c^2+3d^2)+3b^2(c^2+3d^2)\\ N(\alpha)N(\beta)&=(a^2+3b^2)(c^2+3d^2).\end{aligned*} \end{equation*}
So there's that done.
