Say we have internal direct sum decompositions \(R = I_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus I_m\) and \(S = J_1 \oplus \cdots \oplus J_n\) involving minimal left ideals. Then for all \(a\) and \(b\text{,}\) \(I_a \times \{0\}\) and \(\{0\} \times J_b\) are minimal left ideals of \(R \times S\) and they determine an internal direct sum decomposition of \(R \times S\text{.}\)
