Let \(k\) be a field and \(k[x]\) be the ring of polynomials with coefficients in \(k\) in one variable \(x\text{.}\) If \(M\) a finitely generated \(k[x]\)-module then
\begin{equation*} M \cong k[x]^r \oplus k[x]/(p_1^{e_1}) \oplus \cdots \oplus k[x]/(p_s^{e_s}) \end{equation*}
for some \(r \geq 0\text{,}\) \(s \geq 0\text{,}\) non-constant monic, irreducible polynomials \(p_1, \dots, p_s\) and integers \(e_1, \dots, e_s\) with \(e_i \geq 1\text{,}\) and these are unique up to ordering.
