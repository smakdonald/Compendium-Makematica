Theorem 17.16. RCF and Cokernels.

Let \(F\) be a field and let \(A \in \Mat_{n \times n}(F)\text{.}\) The matrix \(xI_n - A \in \Mat_{n \times n}(F[x])\) presents the \(F[x]\)-module \(F^n_A\text{;}\) that is, there is an isomorphism of \(F[x]\)-modules
\begin{equation*} F^n_A \cong \coker(xI_n -A) = F[x]^n/\im(xI_n - A). \end{equation*}

Proof.
