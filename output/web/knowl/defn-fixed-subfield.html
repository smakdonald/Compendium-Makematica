For a field \(L\) and a subgroup \(G\) of \(\Aut(L)\)[^1], the \(\defnn{subfield of}\) \(L\) \(\defnn{fixed by}\) \(G\text{,}\) denoted \(L^G\text{,}\) is by definition
\begin{equation*} L^G := \{ \a \in L \mid \s(\a) = \a, \text{ for all }\s \in G\}. \end{equation*}
\(L^G\) really is a subfield of \(L\) since \(L^G = \bigcap_{\s \in G} L^\s\) where \(L^\s = \{\a \in L \mid \s(\a) = \a\}\text{,}\) and we proved before that \(L^\s\) is a subfield of \(L\text{.}\)
