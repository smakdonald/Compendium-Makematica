Item 4.a.
Let \(G=S_3\text{,}\) \(H=\langle(12)\rangle\text{,}\) and \(K=\langle(13)\rangle\text{.}\) Notice that \(H\cap K=\{e\}\text{,}\) and thus \(|HK|=4\text{,}\) ([provisional cross-reference: empty]) which does not divide \(6\text{.}\) Thus \(HK\) is not a subgroup of \(G\text{,}\) as this would contradict Lagrange's Theorem.
