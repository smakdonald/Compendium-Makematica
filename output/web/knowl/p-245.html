  1. The identity map is a group homomorphism for any group \(G\text{.}\)
  2. The exponential map \(\exp: ({\mathbb{R}}, +) \to ({\mathbb{R}}\setminus \{0\}, \cdot)\) is a homomorphism.
  3. The natural log map \(\ln: ({\mathbb{R}}_{>0}, \cdot) \to ({\mathbb{R}}, +)\) is a homomorphism.
  4. For any positive integer \(n\) the map determinant map \(\det: \operatorname{GL}_n(\C) \to (\C^\times, \cdot)\) is a group homomorphism.
