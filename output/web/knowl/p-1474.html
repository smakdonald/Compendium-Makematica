Let \(rP\in R/P\text{,}\) meaning \(r\not\in P\text{.}\) Then \((rP)^n=r^nP=rP\) for some \(n>1\text{.}\) As \(R/P\) is a domain  4  we can cancel an \(r\) to see that \(r^{n-1}P=P\text{,}\) and thus \(rP\cdot r^{n-2}P=P\text{,}\) making \(rP\) a unit in \(R/P\text{.}\) Thus \(R/P\) is a field, making \(P\) maximal in \(R\text{.}\)
