More generally, let \(V\) be an \(F\)-vector space of dimension \(n\text{,}\) and let \(g: V \to V\) be a linear transformation. The \(\defnn{minimum polynomial}\) of \(g\text{,}\) denoted \(\mp_g(x)\text{,}\) is the unique monic polynomial generating the ideal \(\{f(x) \in F[x] \mid f(g) = 0\}\) or, equivalently, the annihilator ideal \({\ann}_{F[x]}(V_g)\text{.}\)
