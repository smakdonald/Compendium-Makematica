Remark 1.33.

A brief note on notation before we progress to the exercises and beyond. At various points throughout this text, the author may use "mathcal" notation for definitions that do not normally require it (such as \(\G\) for a group, \(\R\) for a ring, etc.) In the realm of higher mathematics, this is what is known as a typo, and should be reported to the proper authorities as soon as possible.
