Exercise 1. Problem 1.

  1. Let \(G\) be a (not necessarily finite) group and \(K \leq G\) a subgroup of index \(n<\infty\text{.}\) Define
\begin{equation*} N=\bigcap_{g \in G} g K g^{-1} \end{equation*}
(i.e., \(N\) is the interesection of all the conjugates of \(K\) ).
(a) (10 points) Prove \(N\) is a the largest normal subgroup of \(G\) that is contained in \(K\text{.}\)
(b) (10 points) Prove \([G: N]\) divides \(n\) !.
