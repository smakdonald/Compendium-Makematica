Example 16.3. Field Extensions.

\(\Q \subseteq \R\) and \(\R \subseteq \C\) are basic examples of field extensions.
Recall [provisional cross-reference: empty] that \(\Q[\sqrt{2}] = \{a + b \sqrt{2} \mid a, b \in \Q\}\) is a field. So \(\Q \subseteq \Q[\sqrt{2}]\) is another example of a field extension.
