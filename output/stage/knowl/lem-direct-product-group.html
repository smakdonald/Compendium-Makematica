Lemma 1.10.

Let \((G,+)\) and \((H,\times)\) be groups. Then \(G\times H\) is a group, where \((G\times H,*)\) denotes the Cartesian Product of \(G\) and \(H\text{,}\) and the operation \(*\) is defined
\begin{equation*} (g_1,h_1)*(g_2,h_2)=(g_1+g_2,h_1\times h_2). \end{equation*}

Proof.
