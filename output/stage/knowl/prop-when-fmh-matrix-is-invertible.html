Proposition 13.36. When FMH Matrix is Invertible.

If \(f: V \to W\) is an isomorphism of free modules of finite rank, \(B\) is an ordered basis of \(V\) and \(C\) is an ordered basis of \(W\text{,}\) then \([f]_B^C\) is an invertible matrix and
\begin{equation*} [f^{-1}]_C^B = \left([f]_B^C\right)^{-1}. \end{equation*}
