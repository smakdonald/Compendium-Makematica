Example 1.46. Common Subgroups.

  1. \(\{e_G\}\) and \(G\) are the trivial subgroups of \(G\text{.}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \Z\leq\Q\leq\R\leq\C\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \Q^\times\leq\R^\times\leq\C^\times\)
  4. Let \(n\in\N\) and define \(n\Z=\{x\in\Z|x=nk\;\text{for some}\;k\in\Z\}\text{.}\) Then \(n\Z\leq\Z\) for all \(n\text{.}\)
