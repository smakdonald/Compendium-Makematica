Proposition 16.68. Prime Fields and Characteristic.

Let \(F\) be a field.
  1. The characteristic \(\char(F)\) is either \(0\) or a prime number \(p\text{.}\)
  2. \(\char(F) = 0\) if and only if the prime subfield of \(F\) is isomorphic to \(\Q\text{;}\) \(\char(F) = p\) for a prime integer \(p\) if and only if the prime subfield of \(F\) is isomorphic to \(\Z/p\text{.}\)
